Yarn Hugs: Hand-Knit Unique Plush Toys for Heartwarming Moments

Meet our adorable characters, each crafted with love and care to bring heartwarming moments to your life.

Professor Purlington

Professor Purlington is the epitome of wisdom and intellect. With his round spectacles and a charming book in hand, he's always ready to share fascinating stories and spark imaginative adventures.

Lawyer Loomis

Lawyer Loomis is a master of arguments and negotiations. With a miniature briefcase by his side, he's the perfect companion for those seeking sage advice or simply a friendly ear to listen.

Roofing Rosie

Roofing Rosie is the expert on all things architecture and roofing. With her hard hat and a tape measure, she's the go-to plush toy for handy advice and construction know-how.
